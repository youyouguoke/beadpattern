Summer Blossom Loom Pattern

A sophisticated loom weaving pattern inspired by Mediterranean summer gardens. Designed for professional finish and effortless color transitions.

Grid Size 48 x 120
Difficulty
Intermediate
Bead Count 5,760
Technique Loom

Palette (12 Colors)

Crafting Instructions

1

Setup Your Loom

Warp your loom with 49 threads. We recommend using a high-tension silk or bonded nylon thread for durability.

2

The First Row

Start from the bottom-left corner. The pattern uses Miyuki Delica 11/0 beads for the most consistent finish.

3

Finishing

Tuck your remaining thread ends back through 3-4 rows of beads to ensure the pattern doesn't unravel over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What bead brand should I use? expand_more

This pattern is specifically optimized for Miyuki Delica 11/0 beads. Their perfectly cylindrical shape ensures the grid remains precise. Using rounded seed beads may cause the pattern to warp.

Is the thread count included in the PDF? expand_more

Yes! The downloadable PDF includes a full bead legend, row-by-row word chart, and a visual high-resolution grid for reference.

Can I use this for Peoyte stitch? expand_more

This is a square-grid loom pattern. While it can be adapted for Peyote stitch, the alignment will shift by half a bead per row. We recommend using our Generator to convert this to a Peyote-specific grid.

Mastering Loom Weaving with BeadPatternAI

Loom weaving is one of the oldest and most satisfying methods of beadwork. Our AI-generated patterns, like the Summer Blossom, are designed to take the guesswork out of complex designs.
